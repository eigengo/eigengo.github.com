Training ANNs

  • Many “nearly optimal” solutions
  • Optimization and training techniques—LBFGS, back–propagation, batch and online gradient descent, downpour SGD, sandblaster LBFGS

Back–propagation recipe

  • Use ReLU units
  • Use cross–entropy loss for classification
  • Use Stochastic gradient descent on mini–batches
  • Shuffle training samples
  • Normalize input (zero mean, unit variance)
  • Schedule to decrease the learning rate
  • Add a bit of L1 and L2 regularisation (after a few epochs)
  • Use drop–out for regularisation
