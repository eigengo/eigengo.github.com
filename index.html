    Instrument Akka & Play applications

    Reactive Monitor

    Use our monitor to keep an eye on a debugged, tuned Akka system that is running in production. In production, you may want to monitor the actors' queues, the number of messages they process, the performance of the receive function and error rates. The Reactive Monitor does just that without any code change to your application.

    Jan Machacek | Pointy-haired techie and competitive cyclist

    Jan Machacek

    Jan is a passionate technologist with hands-on experience with the practical aspects of software delivery (architecture, quality, CI, CD); the project management approaches (applying the principles of agile project management); mentoring and motivating the engineering & business teams. He shares his expertise and passion for software as the editor of the Open Source Journal, regularly contributes to open source projects and speaks at conferences in the UK and abroad. Jan is the author of many open source projects (various Typesafe Activators, Reactive Monitor, Akka Patterns, Akka Extras, Scalad, Specs2 Spring and others), books and articles.

    Jan Machacek is always keen to hear about interesting projects, events, hackathons, ...! He's always keen to share his expertise at conferences, user groups, or simply over a drink or two. If you have something to suggest or share, get in touch!
